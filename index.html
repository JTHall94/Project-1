Limitless Justice for a Justice-less World

Since moving to Lexington in 2014, I have come to love this city. It has everything you could ever want: farms, horses, churches, basketball, and even a Burlington Coat Factory. The people here are really nice actually, and not at all like what I always thought Kentuckians would be like.

There's lots to love about Lexington, but as I've gotten to know it over time, I've also realized that there is lots of CRIME. That's why I'm running for Constable, and why I need your vote. I, Michael Gary Scott, will bring justice to this beautiful city.
